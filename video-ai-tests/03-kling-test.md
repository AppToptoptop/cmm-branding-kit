# Kling AI — Test Guide

**Type** : Text-to-Video (long form)
**Prix** : Free tier disponible
**URL** : https://klingai.com
**Avantage** : Vidéos plus longues, motion réaliste

---

## Use Cases CMM

1. **Scènes longues** — séquences de 10+ secondes
2. **Mouvement réaliste** — personnages qui bougent naturellement
3. **Narration visuelle** — mini-stories sans cut

---

## Test 1 : Morning Routine Sequence

### Prompt

```
A woman in her 30s wakes up in a bright bedroom. Her phone rings on the nightstand. She reaches over, picks it up, and answers with a warm smile. Morning sunlight through curtains. Realistic movement, cinematic lighting. 10 seconds continuous shot.
```

### Paramètres

| Paramètre | Valeur |
|-----------|--------|
| Duration | 10 seconds |
| Quality | High |
| Motion mode | Realistic |

---

## Test 2 : Kitchen Check-in

### Prompt

```
A woman in a modern kitchen preparing healthy breakfast. Her phone is propped up on the counter, showing an active call with speaker on. She talks while cooking, nodding and smiling. Natural domestic scene, warm morning light. Documentary style.
```

---

## Test 3 : Walking & Talking

### Prompt

```
A woman walking in a sunny park, phone to her ear, having an animated conversation. She laughs, nods, looks motivated. Tracking shot following her. Golden hour lighting, lifestyle aesthetic. 8 seconds.
```

---

## Test 4 : Before/After Lifestyle

### Prompt

```
Time-lapse style: A cluttered desk with ignored phone notifications transforms into an organized workspace with a woman confidently answering a call. Day progresses from dark to bright. Symbolizing transformation. 6 seconds.
```

## Kling vs Runway

| Critère | Kling | Runway |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Durée max | 10 sec | 4 sec |
| Free tier | Oui | Limité |
| Mouvement humain | Excellent | Bon |
| Qualité artistique | Bon | Excellent |
| Vitesse | Lent | Rapide (Turbo) |
