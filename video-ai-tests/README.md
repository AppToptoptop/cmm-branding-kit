# Call Me Maybe — Video AI Tools Testing

Guide pour tester les outils de génération vidéo AI avec des prompts CMM.

---

## Prompt de test unifié

Utiliser le même prompt sur chaque plateforme pour comparer les résultats.

### Prompt principal (Phone Call Scene)

```
A smartphone on a wooden nightstand at 6:30 AM. Soft golden morning light through white curtains. The phone screen lights up showing an incoming call with "Your AI Coach" displayed. The phone starts vibrating gently. Cinematic, warm colors, shallow depth of field. 4 seconds.
```

### Prompt secondaire (Transformation)

```
Split screen: Left side shows a tired woman looking at her phone with notifications she ignores. Right side shows the same woman, energized, answering a phone call with a smile. Morning bedroom setting, natural light. Cinematic transition between the two sides. 6 seconds.
```

### Prompt témoignage (pour HeyGen)

```
A French woman in her 30s, casual home setting, speaking directly to camera with a warm smile. She says: "Avant, j'ignorais toutes les notifications. Maintenant, je décroche chaque matin. Et ça change tout." Natural lighting, authentic feel.
```
