## 1. Beta Tester Profile

### Ideal Beta Tester
- **Age:** 25-45
- **Location:** France (for French-language coaching)
- **Goal:** Wants to lose 5-20kg
- **Tech comfort:** Uses smartphone daily, comfortable with phone calls
- **Availability:** Can answer a 3-minute call every morning for 14+ days
- **Communication:** Willing to give honest feedback (not just "it's great")
- **Bonus:** Active on social media (potential future testimonials)

### Anti-Profile (Avoid)
- People who just want free stuff with no intent to engage
- Anyone with a history of eating disorders (we're not equipped for clinical support)
- People who won't answer daily calls
- Technical testers only interested in finding bugs (we need user experience feedback)

## 11. Post-Beta Actions

### With the Data
1. Compile all feedback into a single "Beta Learnings" document
2. Calculate final NPS and satisfaction scores
3. Identify top 3 product improvements for launch
4. Create case studies from best results (with consent)
5. Pull 5-10 testimonial quotes for landing page / App Store

### With the Testers
1. Send personalized thank-you email to each tester
2. Deliver promised rewards (free access, badges, pizza)
3. Invite all to Ambassador program (if qualified)
4. Ask for App Store reviews on launch day
5. Ask to share launch on social media (provide ready-made posts)

### For Launch Readiness
- [ ] All critical bugs from beta are fixed
- [ ] Voice prompts updated based on beta feedback
- [ ] Call flow optimized based on average call duration data
- [ ] FAQ updated with questions from beta testers
- [ ] 10+ testimonials ready for marketing use
- [ ] Beta completion rate >70% (validates product-market fit signal)
