The problem

You've tried every app. You used them for a week. They're still sending notifications you swipe away.

We all do it. 92% of resolutions fail. Fitness apps, diet trackers, habit apps -- they all wait for you to open them. And you don't.

The problem was never knowledge. It was follow-through. What if instead of another app you forget, something actually reached out to you -- a message, a voice note, or even a phone call -- every single day?

What we're building

It reaches out. You respond. You move forward.

1
Pick your goal -- lose weight, get fit, get better at a sport, build a habit, or hit any personal objective.
2
Pick how you want to interact -- reply to a Telegram or WhatsApp message, send a voice note, or go all-in with a live phone call. Your choice, your time.
3
3-5 minutes, every day -- the coach reviews yesterday, asks the right questions, adjusts your plan. It learns your patterns, your triggers, your weak spots. Day 30, it knows you better than you know yourself.
4
You act -- summary, streak, progress graph. The longer you use it, the more personalized it gets. Perfect memory, zero judgment.

The idea: it's not another app you open -- it's a daily conversation that comes to you. Whether you type a quick reply, send a voice note, or pick up a real phone call -- you show up every day. That's 2.7x more effective than any app you've ever downloaded.

The 5 key features

Not just a call. A complete system.

📞
The daily AI check-in -- Your phone actually rings (or you get a Telegram/WhatsApp message). The AI asks 3 questions: what did you eat? Did you move? How are you feeling? Phone calls increase adherence by 78% vs control groups.
🎤
Voice food logging -- "I had a chicken salad and a coffee" -- the AI understands, parses, and logs it. Zero friction. The #1 problem with MyFitnessPal and Noom is manual logging. We eliminate it.
🚨
The SOS button -- You're standing in front of the vending machine. One tap, the AI calls you back in 5 seconds. "Hey, what's happening right now? Let's talk." In-the-moment intervention. NoFap's emergency button gets 50K monthly visitors -- the demand is massive.
📈
Weight loss timeline predictor -- "You'll reach 75kg by July 14th, 2026." A graph with weekly milestones, updated with your real results. This is Noom's highest-converting screen (+40% conversion).
🔥
Streak system with forgiveness -- Call streak, goal streak, partner streak. One free streak freeze per month. Users with 7+ day streaks are 3.6x more likely to stay. Duolingo's streak freeze reduced churn by 21%.

The name

Two names. One brand. One coach persona.

We need two distinct names: the app name (what shows on your phone, in the App Store) and the coach name (who you hear when you pick up). Like Duolingo (app) and Duo (mascot). This lets us add multiple coach personas without rebranding.
