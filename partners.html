Questions

Frequently Asked

How much does it cost?

+
Nothing. You only provide the perk (product, discount, or service). There are no advertising fees, no monthly subscription, and no hidden costs.

What kind of perks work best?

+
Physical products (food, drinks), experiences (gym passes, wellness sessions), and discounts (10–20% off) perform best. The more tangible and immediately rewarding, the higher the engagement.

How do I get the SEO backlink?

+
Once approved, we add your business to our partner directory with a dofollow link to your website. This link is permanent as long as the partnership is active.

Who are CMM users?

+
Health-conscious French adults (25–40), primarily women, focused on weight loss and habit building. They are engaged, motivated, and actively looking for products that support their wellness journey.
