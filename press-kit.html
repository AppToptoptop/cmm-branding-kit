01 / Company
Company Overview

Short Description 50 words — Copy-Paste Ready

Call Me Maybe is an AI accountability coach that proactively calls users every morning for weight loss. Unlike apps you forget to open, we call you. 87% daily engagement rate vs 15–20% for traditional health apps.

Long Description 150 words — For Articles

95% of diets fail. Not because people lack willpower, but because they lack accountability. The average health app loses 77% of users within 3 days. People download, swipe, forget. Call Me Maybe flips the model: instead of waiting for users to open an app, an AI coach calls them every morning by phone. A 3-minute daily check-in covers weight, meals, mood, and goals. The AI adapts its tone—supportive after a bad day, challenging when you need a push. Think of it as a personal coach in your pocket for €9.90/month—10x cheaper than human coaching. The company targets the €405B global weight loss market, launching France-first where zero competitors combine AI phone calls with weight loss coaching. Founded in 2026 in Paris by Aaron Besnainou (Product & Marketing) and Ata (Backend & Strategy), Call Me Maybe is building the future of proactive health AI.

07 / FAQ
Frequently Asked by Journalists

How is this different from Noom?

+
Noom is a content-based app: articles, quizzes, food logging. Users must remember to open it, and 77% don't after 3 days. Call Me Maybe is the opposite—we proactively call you by phone every morning. You don't open an app; you answer a call. The AI adapts to your tone, tracks your progress by voice, and costs €9.90/month vs Noom's €50+/month. Noom is a reading assignment. CMM is a phone call from someone who cares.

Why phone calls instead of an app?

+
Apps rely on users remembering to open them. Phone calls don't. When your phone rings at 8am, you answer. That's 87% daily engagement vs 15-20% for the best health apps. Research shows that phone-based coaching produces 56% more weight loss than self-directed apps. Calls create a human-like commitment loop: someone is expecting you. You show up. The call is only 3 minutes—shorter than scrolling Instagram—but it creates a daily anchor that drives lasting behavior change.

Is the AI really calling by phone?

+
Yes. A real phone call to your actual phone number. No app download required to receive the call. We use VAPI.ai for voice synthesis and Twilio for telephony. The AI speaks naturally, listens to your responses, and adapts in real-time. It sounds like a supportive human coach, not a robotic IVR. Users consistently report forgetting they're talking to AI within the first 30 seconds of the call.

What about privacy and RGPD compliance?

+
Privacy is foundational, not an afterthought. All data is stored on EU servers (RGPD-compliant). Call recordings are processed for coaching insights then encrypted. Users own their data and can export or delete it anytime. We never sell data to third parties. Health data is treated with the same sensitivity as medical records. Users provide explicit consent for each data type. Our privacy policy is transparent and written in plain language, not legalese.

What’s your business model?

+
B2C subscription: €9.90/month for Pro (daily calls, voice food logging, streak system, weekly reports). Free tier with 3 calls/week. Duo plan at €14.90/month for couples/friends. B2B channel: white-label for mutuelles (health insurers), corporate wellness programs, and gym chains. Partner referral commissions (25% for dietitians). Unit economics: €1.20 COGS per call, €4.80 CAC target, 8-month path to profitability. LTV:CAC ratio target of 4:1.
