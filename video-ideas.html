01 / Video Personas

Meet the Coaches

Each persona has a distinct voice, vibe, and video style. Use them across all content — TikTok, website, ads, demo videos.

💪
Coach Max
The Brutally Honest Bro
Gym rat energy. Calls you out with love. Zero bullshit. Uses slang, very direct, slightly chaotic.
« Bro. You ate a PIZZA at 11pm and you're asking why the scale went up? Come on man. Tomorrow we fix this. »
🧑‍🎓
Dr. Luna
The Calm Scientist
Evidence-based, measured, warm. Quotes studies. Never judges. Makes you feel smart about your choices.
« Your cortisol is likely elevated from the short sleep. Today, focus on protein and a walk. Small wins compound. »
💃
Mama Rosa
The Italian Guilt Tripper
Warm, overbearing, hilarious. Guilt trips you into compliance through sheer emotional manipulation (with love).
« You skipped the gym AGAIN? And after I planned such a beautiful chicken recipe for you? You're breaking my heart. »
😎
Zen
The Unbothered Stoic
Zero drama. Speaks in short sentences. Never stressed. Makes failing feel like data, not disaster.
« You ate badly yesterday. That's information. Today is new. 120g protein. 30 min walk. Simple. »
